About Vishuddh Herbals

Pure. Natural. Wholesome – Crafted by Nature, Perfected by Us.

Our Story

Vishuddh Herbals began with a simple mission – to bring the purity of nature into everyday self-care. Our journey started in small herbal gardens, blending age-old Ayurvedic wisdom with modern formulations to create safe, sustainable, and effective products for all.

Our Philosophy

We believe true beauty comes from nature’s simplicity. Each product is made with ethically sourced ingredients, free from harsh chemicals, parabens, and synthetic fragrances. We ensure every formula respects both your skin and the planet.

Nature’s Finest Ingredients

Tulsi

Known for its cleansing and healing properties, Tulsi rejuvenates and purifies the skin naturally.

Aloe Vera

Deeply hydrates and nourishes the skin, giving it a soothing and refreshing feel.

Neem

A powerful antibacterial herb that promotes clear, healthy, and balanced skin.

Asvgranda

A revitalizing herb that helps reduce stress, supports skin nourishment, and promotes a natural, healthy glow.

Ginseng

A rejuvenating root known for boosting skin vitality, improving circulation, and enhancing the skin’s natural radiance.

Usiri (Amla)

Rich in Vitamin C and antioxidants, Usiri deeply nourishes the skin and hair, promoting natural glow and strengthening roots.

Our Commitment to Sustainability

Every product we create honors our commitment to sustainability. From biodegradable packaging to cruelty-free testing and eco-conscious sourcing, Vishuddh Herbals strives to preserve the planet while nourishing your well-being.
